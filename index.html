Who We Are
We are the only Japanese language training institute in Sri Lanka that employs native Japanese teachers who are adeptly qualified by the Japanese Ministry of Education and possess hands-on experience for conducting our lessons with the aim of achieving the most optimum results from our students.
What We Do
We at Kayo School Of Japanese recruit students from Sri Lanka to study at reputable and renowned Japanese language schools across Japan . We also offer students the choice to attend Vocational institutions and Universities which offer a plethora of courses in the English language .
