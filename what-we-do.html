Work Part-time to earn while studying
As per the Japanese laws and regulations we provide part time jobs in super markets , convenience stores , recycle shops , courier companies and restaurants.These part time jobs not only help to earn sufficient amounts of money but also enable students to practice their Japanese language skills in the real world . The part time job wages start from a minimum of 850 JPY to a maximum of 1300 JPY per hour.
