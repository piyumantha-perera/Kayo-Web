Our History
Since its inception in November 2011, Kayo International LLC has been successfully flourishing in its services for providing superior quality education in teaching Japanese to numerous Sri Lankans thereby enabling their aspirations of creating a brighter future in Japan .

We are affiliated with many reputed Japanese language schools , top ranking Japanese medium universities and world ranked English medium universities across japan. Hence we are dynamically keen on offering a substantially improved learning experience for all our students .
Kayo School of Japanese

Kayo School of Japanese is a product of collaboration founded by a native Japanese and Sri Lankan entrepreneur who wants to bridge the knowledge gap between Sri Lanka and Japan.
Why Choose Kayo?

Kayo International LLC offers concise courses in JLPT N5 , JLPT N4 and JLPT N3 for those who wish to improve their Japanese language proficiency. Therefore, apart from conducting stringent tutorial sessions complete with mock examinations including that with past papers under the supervision of qualified educators all the way from Japan.

We also offer visas for prosective students along with placements at Japan's top Techincal Colleges and universities. Not only do we provide the necessary guidance to attain a quality education in Japan , but we also look after our students post arrival . We offer secure and safe accommodation which is affordable to all students . Furthermore students do not have to depend on their parents for financial necessities when in Japan , as we will be providing part time jobs which will generate sufficient amounts of income for students to survive.
